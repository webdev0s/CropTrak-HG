Grow smarter with a digital supply chain built for you

When it comes to modern Food & Agriculture data management, FMIS is only the beginning. CropTrak offers a flexible suite of solutions that allows us to assemble and configure a system that offers exactly what you need, with zero waste or extraneous functionality.

Field & Asset Management

Define and document your fields, assets and production units in both geospatial and list view format. Pinpoint location, status and user group hierarchy according to your supply management process. CropTrak enables you and your teams to visualize, share and manage field assets the way you want to.

Field Management Configurator

Digitize your business processes by configuring CropTrak using our interactive form and workflow design tools. Take advantage of our best practice library of prebuilt forms, workflows, and crop-specific logic to easily create task lists, define user interactions, and connect to data or files from in-house spreadsheets or external databases.

Trials Management

We support some of the most sophisticated performance and efficacy trials in the food and ag industry. We configure trial-specific forms and workflows, allowing dynamic monitoring of locations, protocols, progress and results, all in a single platform with source data verification.

Field & Production Monitoring

Easily visualize your entire crop season. Collaborate to track and report on inputs, and activities across your entire production portfolio using integrated web and mobile workflows designed for your organization and field personnel.

Supply Planning & Allocation

With the complex seasonality inherent in food production, visibility of crop production estimates and supply inventory in real time is critical. CropTrak enables your operation to stay on pace and adjust early and often to market and weather dynamics. Combined with our pre-season planting and supply allocation simulators, we have you covered.

Smart QR Data Entry

We've developed our own unique automated data management application to track the origin and journey of your crops through harvest, delivery and receiving. CropTrak can give each crop a digital identity that can be assigned at the field, block, or sub-block level depending on your needs. Then, automate the transfer of data to forms through our proprietary scannable QR technology.

Digital Contracting

Transform your outdated, paper contract process into a transparent and connected digital experience between you, your teams, and your growers. We combine our configurable approach with innovative technology to create a modern user experience for your contract growers and suppliers. Imagine, monitoring fields and managing growers together in one simple to use system.

Weights, Grades & Payments

From field to harvest to delivery. A natural extension of our digital contracting solution, we also collect, process and report at the scale including weights, grades and settlements. Each process is custom tailored to your requirements, business rules and contract specifications.

ERP Integration

We develop and customize ERP specific integrations allowing automated, secure data transfer from contract settlements at the point of delivery. Save time, reduce data entry error, and automate settlements to invoices, strictly adhering to your business rules and requirements.

Compliance & Audit Reports

With your instance of CropTrak software, your diverse workflow data resides on a common platform, enabling you to easily report on Environmental, Social and Governance (ESG) requirements critical to your organization, all with source data verification.

ESG Survey Management

With flexible forms technology we've created templates for data collection not only within your organization, but outside as well. ESG surveys can be easily converted to web or mobile forms and quickly sent to internal and external users. The result? Easily collect and validate mission-critical data for your sustainability initiatives, directly and efficiently.

Data Insights & Analytics

With CropTrak, your data is always yours, and it's easily accessible, either through our preconfigured performance dashboards or through any compatible analytical tool of your choice. We understand data is only as good as the decisions it can support, and our deployments are always configured with that as a guiding principle.

Adaptable to any ecosystem

We recognize that collaboration is critical to any successful software implementation. Our software is designed to coexist with other technology platforms and fully integrate with many of the most common F&A support tools.
