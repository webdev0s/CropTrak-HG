A shift in perspective

CropTrak is changing the way we think about the supply chain. Traditionally thought of as a cost of doing business, we’re proving that innovative supply chain management can be a competitive advantage and differentiator for brands.

Producing change over time

Our mission is focused on enhancing food supply chain integrity, and we’ve been proudly doing just that for nearly 15 years. Our software solutions connect some of the world’s largest food and agriculture companies with their suppliers and growers to collect, manage, process, and report data and metrics on crop systems, supplier contracts, financial settlements, and sustainability initiatives.

Enhancing food supply chain integrity together

Our strategic partners passionately share our commitment. Not only are International Farming and Sompo International respective leaders in the farming, food and agriculture industry, they’re CropTrak customers.

International Farming

International Farming (I.F.) is a leading food-sourcing solutions platform. Through innovative ag tech, asset management and strategic partnerships, I.F. empowers food buyers and farmers to build a more transparent, global food economy. With agricultural roots dating back to 1827, I.F. brings a deep understanding and respect for farming and the land to its investment strategy.

Sompo International

Through its operating subsidiaries, Sompo International Holdings Ltd (an indirect wholly owned subsidiary of Sompo Holdings, Inc) is a leading global provider of commercial and consumer property and casualty (re)insurance. With approximately 10,000 employees at Sompo International, 75,000 employees total worldwide, entities in over 29 countries and over $36 billion in Gross Written Premiums, Sompo Holdings is one of the leading property and casualty insurance groups in the world.
